Our Story

With every street-style girl in the wild pursuit of a vintage dream, there is the thrill of the chase. The Vintage Store is here to help you chase the best vintage treasures.​ Since the beginning of 2018, The Vintage Store has become known as a Danish vintage handbag specialist. Specializing in hand-selected vintage bags, we believe that great style should be accessible to everyone, so we strive to create a shopping universe that is both inviting and with affordable prices.

Our commitment is to find new lovely owners for each piece in our selection of pre-loved vintage bags – that are ready to get loved again. We strive to give our customers an unforgettable shopping experience, which is why we do our ultimate best to guide our customers to find their perfect match in their search for a new vintage lover.

Our vision is to become a major reference in the world of luxury vintage products without forgetting about our mission: to hand-pick the best selection of pre-loved designer items and making these creations accessible to everyone by delivering an effortless, safe and affordable online shopping experience.
