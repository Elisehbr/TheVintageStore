Balenciaga First

kr. 4.800,00

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Balenciaga’s Motorcycle Bags, now a cult favorite, almost never made it to retail. Designed in 2001 by Nicolas Ghesquiere, the fashion house originally only approved the production of 25 sample pieces, which were sent exclusively to fashion editors and models. Kate Moss very publicly declared the City her favorite…and the rest is history.

Mixing soft, lightweight leather with tough details, the Motorcycle Bags are both feminine and edgy. This First is crafted of lambskin leather, and features antique brass studs and buckles, tassel zipper pulls, whipstitched top handles, a pocket mirror, and a detachable shoulder strap. If you’re an avid concert-goer, it is a must-have. The sized-down version of the City, the First is large enough to fit your phone, wallet, and ticket, but small enough to sling over your shoulder while you dance along to the set. In Ardoise, a medium gray color, this First is a neutral that goes with everything. No matter what outfit you pair it with, it will always give you the ‘I’m with the band’, rocker-chic look.

  • The exterior leather is in fine condition with minor marks and scuffs.
  • The interior lining is in good condition with minor signs of use.
  • The leather handles have darkened from use.
