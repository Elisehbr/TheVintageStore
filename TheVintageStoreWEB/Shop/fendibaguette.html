Fendi Shoulder Bag

kr. 6.000,00

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

It took Karl Lagerfeld only 5 seconds to design Fendi’s ‘FF’ logo, referred to as the Zucca. Standing for ‘Fun Fur’, it was originally intended to grace only the silk lining of fur coats, but now covers everything. This shoulder bag is crafted of black Zucca canvas and rhodinated silver hardware. It features black leather detailing, buckle detailing, and an interior zipper pocket. You do not have to wear a fur to get the Fendi look!

  • The exterior canvas is clean and in good condition.
  • The interior lining is clean and in good condition.
  • The leather handles have some sign of use.
  • The silver hardware has minor surface scratches.
