PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Gucci’s Accessory Collection was produced, from the late ’70s through the ’90s, by the brand’s perfumery department. This crossbody, from that exclusive collection, is crafted of brown coated canvas that is detailed with the house’s instantly recognizable Diamante pattern and red-green web. It features gold hardware and a main zipper compartment. Carrying this crossbody, everyone will know it is a Gucci.
